About

Hi, my name is Guy Rotshtein. I'm 24, live in Herzlyia, and I',currently studying for a first degree at Shenkar College, at their department of engineering, for 3 years. I am proficient with a multitude of programing languages, including java, javascript, html (and css), c++ and more.

During my free time, I like to draw, read, study languages, and play video games with my friends. I also love to learn, with topics ranging from foreign languages, history, random facts, to coding and modifying programs. In the past, I have already dabbled with some coding languages, such as C♯ and Javascript, but never went too deep into them.

During my time of service with the Israeli Defense Force, I served as a computer technician and system admin for the IDF. Afterwards, I spent a couple of years continuing to work in the IT field, before deciding that a change of trajectory in life would be beneficial, and since I was already familiar with coding, I decided to pursue a degree in Computer Engineering, which brought me to Shenkar.
